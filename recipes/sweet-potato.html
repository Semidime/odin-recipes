Sweet Potato Puree

A rich sweet potato puree with roasted garlic and pomegranate molasses.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes.
  • 10 cloves of garlic - unpeeled.
  • 75g of unsalted butter.
  • 1 tsp olive oil.
  • 3 tbsp pomegranate molasses.
  • 1 - 2 tbsp pomegranate seeds - optional.

Method

  1. Preheat overn to 170 degrees centigrade.
  2. Place sweet potatoes on a baking tray and roast for 45 minutes
  3. Toss garlic cloves in olvice oil, and wrap in a tinfoil pouch. Place pouch on the baking tray with the sweet potatoes and continue to roast for a further 25 minutes.
  4. Remove baking tray from the oven. Sweet potatoes should be very tender, and beginning to caramalise.
  5. Peel sweet potatoes and transfer flesh to a saucepan over a medium heat.
  6. Squeeze roasted garlic cloves into the saucepan and add unsalted butter.
  7. Stir gently with a wooden spoon until consistency is smooth.
  8. Stir through pomegranate molasses.
  9. Transfer to serving dish.
  10. Optional: garnish with pomegranate seeds.
